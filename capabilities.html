Retail & Program Partnerships

We manufacture across hand-tufted, handloom, woven, hand-knotted, and higher-end Tibetan silk constructions, supplying major U.S. retailers including Macy’s, Bloomingdale’s, and Wayfair, alongside our long-standing prime importer of over two decades, Bashian Rugs.

Over the years, we have developed programs across multiple quality tiers — from better tufted and loomed lines to intricate hand-knotted and Nepalese Weave — and have supported private label developments for Macy’s, Bloomingdale’s, and SFERRA.

Our strength lies in balancing scale with discipline — and tradition with evolution.
